About us

Release The Kringle! was founded on March 17, 2021. One of our founders, Brett Kringles, on a rainy night needed to satisfy his hunger, therefore he crawled into Trader Joes and found some Raspberry Kringles. He then inspired us founders to bake Kringles for a living. Our freshly made Kringles from Kalifornia are amazing for the holidays and whenever you crave for sweets. They are great gifts for your loved ones and will continue to be your favorite dessert once you try our Kringles.

Our Kringles are made fresh daily. They are topped with icing glaze with many different flavor fillings that you can check out in our menu. They are 12 inches length and are deliciously made.
